Annual Report 2019 - Beta Company

In 2019, the Company opened 125 new stores, ending the year with 856 stores (720 conventional and 159 virtual), 10 kiosks in addition to our website. Of the total base, 20% of stores are in the process of maturation.

Gross revenue increased 27.0% to R$25.3 billion, due to accelerated growth in e-commerce, increase in same physical store sales plus the contribution of new stores. The gross margin is explained by a significant increase in the share of e-commerce, new categories and the marketplace performance. In 2018 gross revenue was R$ 19.9 billion, R$ 14.9 billion in 2017, R$ 12.1 billion in 2016, and R$ 11.2 billion in 2015. Gross profit reached R$6.8 billion with a stable gross margin of 27.1%. In 2018 it was R$ 5.4 billion, R$ 4.4 billion in 2017, R$ 3.7 billion in 2016, and R$ 3.1 billion in 2015.

In 2019, net income totalled R$784.3 million. In 2015 was R$ -89.6 million, R$ 108.9 million in 2016, R$ 476.8 million in 2017, and R$ 776.1 million in 2018.
